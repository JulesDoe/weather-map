Weather Map on Renewable Energy

https://github.com/rodighiero/weather-map

Tested on Google Chrome

Legenda

RED stays for emerging elements

BLUE stays for disappearing elements

H stays for an area of HIGH activity

L stays for an area of LOW activity

GRAY curved line marks opposed areas

GRAY stays for subjects of debate

GRAY contours stay for actors' trend

GRAY crosses idicate actors' position

By zooming, actors' name is revealed

RED and BLUE crosses reveal actors' trend

By clicling, actors' further info is also revealed
